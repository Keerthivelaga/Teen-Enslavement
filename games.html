Real Incident — Lucknow, India — 2022
Lucknow Boy Kills Mother over PUBG

A 16-year-old boy shot his mother dead after she stopped him from playing PUBG. The juvenile, described by police as severely addicted to the game, used his father's pistol during the late-night hours.


"The police reached the spot after getting information about the incident. During the investigation, it was revealed that the 16-year-old son shot dead his mother. The minor boy shot his mother after she stopped him from playing the PUBG game," said ADCP Qasim Abidi, East Lucknow.


A preliminary probe revealed the boy had been addicted to the game for an extended period, and his mother regularly intervened. He tried to mislead police during investigation by fabricating a story about an electrician. He was taken into custody and further investigation was ordered.

Real Incident — Madhya Pradesh, India — 2022
13-Year-Old Dies by Suicide After Free Fire Losses

A 13-year-old boy in Chhatarpur district took his own life after secretly spending ₹40,000 from his mother's bank account on the online battle royale game Free Fire. The boy and his sister were alone at home when the incident occurred.


His mother, who was at work, received an SMS notification that ₹1,500 had been debited from her bank account. She called her son who confessed to having spent money on the game. After being reprimanded, the boy went to his room and hanged himself from a ceiling fan.


Police investigating the case recovered a suicide note in which the minor confessed to withdrawing ₹40,000 over time and losing all of it while playing Free Fire. The case highlighted the dangers of children having unrestricted access to digital payment systems.

Warning Signs

Signs of Gaming Addiction

01
Preoccupation
Constantly thinking about the game even when not playing; talking about it excessively.
02
Loss of Time
Playing for hours without awareness of how much time has passed; missing meals and sleep.
03
Withdrawal Symptoms
Irritability, anxiety, or aggression when prevented from playing — just like substance withdrawal.
04
Hiding Behaviour
Lying about how much time is spent gaming or making secret in-game purchases.
05
Neglecting Responsibilities
Skipping school, homework, chores, or other obligations in favour of gaming.
06
Tolerance
Needing to play more and more to get the same feeling of excitement or satisfaction.
