Who We Are

A student awareness initiative from SRKR Engineering College dedicated to fighting teen addiction.

SRKR Engineering College

We are a group of students from SRKR Engineering College who built this awareness platform to shine a light on the growing epidemic of teen addiction — from dangerous substances and social media dependency to gaming addiction.

This project was born from a simple belief: that education and awareness are the most powerful tools we have against addiction. By presenting real stories, evidence-based information, and practical recovery resources, we aim to reach teenagers before addiction does.

Our goal is not to scare, but to inform. Not to judge, but to help. We believe every teenager deserves access to information that could save their life — and we built this platform to provide exactly that.
